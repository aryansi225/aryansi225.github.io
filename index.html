ABOUT ME

Hi, I am Aryan Singh. My interests lie in coming up with elegant and innovative solutions for real-world problems that can be solved using technology. My research interests lie in Machine and Deep learning and have worked on multiple projects related to it. I also have an interest in the blockchain and quantum computing fields. I aspire to work in an innovation-driven research environment where individuals are encouraged to take up real-world complex problems so that the solutions can advance the community and impact the world at large.
